# Text-to-SQL Agent 🧠🗄️

Ask questions about the QueryPilot database in plain English — the agent maps your
question to the schema, writes a read-only PostgreSQL query, runs it, and answers from
the result.

Pick one of the starter questions below, or type your own. You can also ask follow-ups
(e.g. "just the top 5", "add their categories") and the agent keeps the context.
